## Key Shopping Patterns

1. **Protein Focus:** Consistently choose lean proteins (99% lean chicken, grass-fed beef)
2. **Low-Fat Dairy:** Preference for low-fat yogurt options
3. **Organic When Available:** Especially for produce (organic green onions) and kids' items
4. **Plant-Based Options:** Regular tofu purchases, plant-based pasta, dairy-free creamer
5. **Bulk Water:** Always 2-4 units of Deer Park water exchange
6. **Kids' Items:** Consistent purchases of Danimals, GoGo squeeZ, and baby snacks
7. **Protein-Enhanced Products:** Protein+ pasta, protein cereals, protein bars

## Brand Loyalty Notes
- **Strong loyalty to:** Nature's Promise (meat), Icelandic Provisions (yogurt), Cooper (cheese), Silk (creamer), Deer Park (water)
- **Consistent secondary brands:** Boar's Head (deli), Happy Baby/Happy Tot (baby food), Kodiak (granola/bars)
- **Budget alternatives used:** Our Brand for eggs, pasta, and some produce items
