## Prompting Guidelines for AI

### When to Check These Items:
1. **Every 3-4 orders:** Do a quick check on paper products and cleaning supplies
2. **Monthly:** Review condiments and pantry basics
3. **When user mentions:**
   - "Big shop" or "stock up"
   - "Having guests"
   - "Running low on..."
   - Specific meals requiring these ingredients

### How to Ask:
- "I notice it's been a while since you ordered [category]. Need any [specific items]?"
- "Planning any cooking this week? Check on olive oil, spices, or baking supplies?"
- "How are you on paper products and cleaning supplies?"
- "Time for a household basics check - need paper towels, toilet paper, or trash bags?"

### Priority Check Items (Most Commonly Needed):
1. Paper towels
2. Toilet paper
3. Trash bags
4. Dish soap
5. Onions
6. Butter
7. Olive oil
8. Coffee
